The Low-Down

The Vista Point is a groove oriented band featuring James Gallardo on bass, Tyler Miller on drums, Eric Shechter on keyboard, and led by Justin Pecot on guitar and vocals. All four members of the group are active musicians playing throughout the central coast area and beyond. We play covers of our favorite bands and musicians, as well as original compositions. Our music doesn’t fit into any one genre, but instead a mix of jazz, rock, reggae, pop, and anything in between. We love playing for an audience, and our mission is to get our listeners grooving along with us. If you like what you hear, come check us out! Everyone except Tyler doesn’t bite. Catch you on the flip side muthafuckas.
