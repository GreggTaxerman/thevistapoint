The Low-Down

e, The Vista Point, strive to produce music that measures up to the following criteria: undeniable groove, catchy melody, and exciting improvisation. We wish to do so for the benefit of our recordings, our audience, and to best express ourselves.

Artists like Prince inspire us with their keen attention toward infectious rhythms. Bands like The Beatles inspire us with their ability to crafttimeless, memorable songs. And jazz greats like Louis Armstrong inspire us with their ability to improvise at the highest level.

We, The Vista Point, dedicate ourselves to these aforementioned ideals. We are confident that with our upcoming spring release “Rotunda,” we will be presenting music that meets our own high standards.
